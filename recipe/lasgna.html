Lasgna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian baked dish made with layers of pasta, savory sauce, cheese, and often meat or vegetables, cooked until rich, tender, and bubbling.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground meat (beef or sausage)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
