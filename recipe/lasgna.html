Lasgna

Description:

Lasagna is a hearty Italian baked dish made with layers of pasta, savory sauce, cheese, and often meat or vegetables, cooked until rich, tender, and bubbling.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground meat (beef or sausage)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta sheets according to the package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground meat with onion and garlic, then add tomato sauce.
  3. Layer pasta, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Bake until the cheese is melted and golden, then let it cool before serving.
