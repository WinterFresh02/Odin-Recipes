Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish made with a baked flatbread crust topped with sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients, creating a flavorful and versatile meal enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Pepperoni
  • Vegetables
  • Mushrooms
  • Herbs

Steps:

  1. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your chosen toppings.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  5. Let the pizza cool slightly, slice, and serve.
